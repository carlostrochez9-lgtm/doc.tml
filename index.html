Role-Based Access & Shift Management

User Roles & Tiers

Create accounts for Full‑Time, On‑Call Level 1, and On‑Call Level 2 staff, with a flexible tier structure for future levels. Staff logins are held in a pending state until a manager approves access.

Scalable tiers · Manual approval · Audit‑ready

Shift Creation & Uniforms

Manually create banquet shifts with dedicated uniform requirements, or upload PDF/Excel event orders to auto‑generate shifts using parsing tools like pdf‑parse and xlsx.

Uniform tabs · File upload · Smart parsing

Publishing & Pickup Control

Publish shifts to specific levels or open them to all staff. Capture exactly who posted each shift and the precise timestamp when it was picked up for full transparency and accountability.

Tier targeting · Mass publish · Time‑stamped logs

Reporting & Operations

Hours & Summaries

Generate detailed hours reports by staff member, event, or date range. Support payroll, invoicing, and internal oversight with reconciled shift‑based hours.

Export‑ready · Staff & event views

Digital Sign‑In Sheets

Automatically create printable PDF sign‑in sheets for each event, including columns for name, role, time in/out, and physical signatures—perfect for on‑site compliance.

PDF generation · Signature columns
